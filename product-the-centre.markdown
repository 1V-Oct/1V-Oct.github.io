        <h2 class="!text-3xl !mt-0">A New Core for Your Rack</h2>
        <p>The Centre redefines what's possible within a single Eurorack module. At its core, it's a high-fidelity wavetable synthesizer, but its true power lies in its <strong>"modular-in-modular"</strong> architecture.</p>
        <p>This concept allows you to build complex patches by connecting virtual modules—oscillators, filters, envelopes, and LFOs—all within The Centre itself. This approach mirrors the workflow of a DAW while retaining the hands-on immediacy of hardware, supporting up to <strong>4-voice polyphony</strong> to transform an entire patch into a multi-voiced instrument.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

      <div class="text-center mb-16">
        <h2 class="text-4xl md:text-5xl font-eraz font-bold tracking-tight">Internal Modules</h2>
        <p class="mt-4 text-lg text-gray-600 max-w-3xl mx-auto">The "modular-in-modular" design allows you to build patches using a variety of internal virtual modules. Here are the key modules available:</p>
      </div>

      <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3 gap-6">
        <!-- WTO -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Wavetable Oscillator</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">WTO</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">The primary sound source. A powerful wavetable synthesizer capable of producing complex stereo sounds. Its pitch can be controlled via the standard note inputs.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- VCO -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>VCO</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">VCO</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">A standard oscillator for generating basic waveforms. It can be used for audio or as a modulation source and features internal CV outputs.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- MID -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>MIDI Interface</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">MID</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">Processes external MIDI messages and converts them into internal Note, Gate, and Velocity signals. It supports single note, polyphonic, and drum rack modes.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- GAT -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Gate Divider</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">GAT</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">A 4-channel clock divider and pattern generator for creating rhythmic triggers and gate sequences.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- ENV -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Envelope</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">ENV</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">A flexible AHDSR envelope used to shape volume or other parameters. It can also be output as a CV signal.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- VCF -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Filter</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">VCF</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">Shapes the tonal characteristics of a sound by removing or boosting certain frequencies.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- VCA -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Amplifier</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">VCA</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">Controls the final volume of a signal, typically modulated by an envelope to create articulated notes.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- MIX -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Mixer</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">MIX</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">A crucial utility for combining multiple audio signals, essential for mixing voices in polyphonic patches.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- LFS -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>LFO Shaper</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">LFS</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">An advanced LFO that uses customizable shapes for complex modulation. Requires a clock signal for synchronization.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- CLK -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Clock</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">CLK</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">An internal clock generator that can be used to set the master tempo (BPM) for other modules within a patch.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- SMP -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Sample Player</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">SMP</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">A module for playing back audio samples. Its pitch can be controlled via the standard note control inputs.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- RNG -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Random Note Gen</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">RNG</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">Generates random notes, requiring a clock signal to produce a new note at a specified interval for generative melodies.</p>
        </div>
        <!-- OUT -->
        <div class="bg-gray-100/50 rounded-xl p-6">
          <h3 class="font-eraz text-xl mb-2 flex items-center justify-between"><span>Output</span><code class="text-sm font-mono bg-gray-200 text-gray-800 rounded px-2 py-1">OUT</code></h3>
          <p class="text-gray-700 text-sm">Routes audio from internal virtual buffers to the four physical VOUT jacks. A patch needs an OUT module to produce sound.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
